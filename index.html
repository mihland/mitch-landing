About

Mitch Mitchell grew up in Sylvester, Georgia, rooted in faith, family, and a work ethic built on dedication. By 37, he'd built Mitchell Pine Straw into a trusted supplier across the Southeast, raising registered Angus cattle and a commercial cow-calf operation through Mitchell Family Farms, while building Peach State Homes and Mitchell Investments & Holdings, a buy-and-hold land investment company.

None of it came easy, and none of it came alone. It came from hard work, answered prayers, and the people God put in his path along the way. For Mitch, the businesses are just the vehicle — the real purpose is providing for his family, treating people right, and being a good steward of what the Lord has given him.

He's not chasing a title or a spotlight. He's building something his family can carry forward — something built on faith first, dedication second, and the belief that if you honor God in what you do, the rest will follow.

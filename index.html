About

Mitch is a 37-year-old business owner from Sylvester, Georgia, where he was raised and still calls home. He runs several family businesses rooted in agriculture and real estate — the kind of work where you show up early, get your hands dirty, and earn what you've got.

He grew up around hard work and learned early that doing the job right matters more than talking about it. He keeps his operations lean, works alongside his team, and believes in treating people the way he'd want to be treated — whether that's a customer, a driver, or a neighbor down the road.

He's a hands-on operator who'd rather be in the field than behind a desk, and he takes pride in building something real that his family can stand behind. When he's not running loads, working cattle, or closing on a piece of land, Mitch is usually thinking about the next thing he can build.
